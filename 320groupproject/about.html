My name is Kelly Tran. I am a Vietnamese-American, born and raised in New Jersey. I currently attend Rutgers University. I am pursuing a degree in Information Technology and Informatics. My other academic interests include economics and psychology -- both of which I am pursuing minors in!

I am also the president of Pi Chapter of Lambda Kappa Sigma at Rutgers University. We are an all-female chapter. Lambda Kappa Sigma is an international, professional fraternity established in 1913 to promote women within the profession of pharmacy. This is our website that I started and help maintain.
