Hi, my name is Kelly Tran! I am a Vietnamese-American, born and raised in New Jersey. I currently attend Rutgers University. I am pursuing a degree in Information Technology and Informatics. I have also completed a minor in psychology and have a particular interest in the social sciences. I think the mind is a fascinating and complex thing, and I enjoy learning about the ways that people think and what motivates certain behaviors. I believe UX/UI design combines technology and social science and that is where my career interests lie, however, I'm pretty open to learning anything and am not limiting myself to my preferences.

I am also the president of Pi Chapter of Lambda Kappa Sigma at Rutgers University. While Lambda Kappa Sigma is a co-ed organization, our chapter at Rutgers University is an all-female chapter. Lambda Kappa Sigma is an international, professional fraternity established in 1913 to promote women within the profession of pharmacy. This is our website that I started and help maintain.
