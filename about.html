Hello! My name is Kelly and I currently attend Rutgers University. I am pursuing a BA in Information Technology and Informatics and a minor in pyschology. I have a particular interest in the social sciences because I enjoy learning about people, what motivates certain behaviors, and mental health. My professional interests lie in UX/UI design because I believe it's the bridge between people and technology.

I am also the president of Pi Chapter of Lambda Kappa Sigma at Rutgers University. While Lambda Kappa Sigma is a co-ed organization, our chapter at Rutgers University is an all-female chapter. Lambda Kappa Sigma is an international, professional fraternity established in 1913 to promote women within the profession of pharmacy. This is our website that I started and help maintain.
