Management of Technological Organizations: Showcase Project


Healthcare IT Team: HelpTech


HelpTech is an IT consulting firm that has been hired by Zocdoc to find a solution to achieve the following directives:

  1. Help doctors promote themselves and connect with more patients in order to increase the value of having a registered account on Zocdoc
  2. Create content that is sticky so users of Zocdoc who come to find a doctor will have a reason to return to use Zocdoc services

What's Up Doc


Our solution is the What's Up Doc app! What's Up Doc can be likened to a social media platform for doctors and members of the community--whether they are current patients, potential patients, or just people who want to learn more about healthcare topics.

Doctors benefit from this app because it helps with community outreach and patient engagement. Our engine also delivers articles and content to the doctors based on their preferences and interests, so they can share and post content that aligns with their interests.
